About NNA Community Broadband

We are a locally operated wireless internet service provider serving the Nenana community. Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable internet access to homes and businesses in the area.

Operated by the Nenana Native Association, we're committed to bridging the digital divide and keeping our community connected.

Why Choose Us?

  • No long-term contracts
  • No hidden fees or data caps
  • Professional installation included
  • Responsive customer service
